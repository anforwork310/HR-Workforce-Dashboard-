# HR-Workforce-Dashboard
An interactive Power BI dashboard providing insights into employee demographics, turnover, satisfaction, and recruitment performance to support strategic HR decisions.

## I. Introduction
### 1. Introduction to Dataset

- The dataset contains detailed HR employee information including demographics, compensation, tenure, satisfaction scores, performance ratings, and recruitment sources.
- It allows analysis across multiple dimensions such as department, gender, and age group to monitor workforce composition, engagement, and retention.

## III. Insights
- Turnover Rate: 34.9% overall, with Production having the highest at 40.89%, followed by Admin and Software Engineering.
- Satisfaction Score: Average of 3.88/5, with lower scores in Admin Offices and Executive roles indicating potential morale issues.
- Employee Profile:
  + Average age: 46.8 years
  + Average tenure: 12.47 years
  + Gender split: 56.7% Female, 43.3% Male
  + Largest age group: 35–44 years (130 employees)
- Recruitment Sources: Majority from Indeed (81) and LinkedIn (73); other channels show minimal contribution.
- Departure Reasons: Top causes include seeking another position (20) and job dissatisfaction (14).

## IV. Recommendations
- Focus Retention Efforts on the Production department through improved working conditions and recognition programs.
- Enhance Employee Engagement by addressing low satisfaction in certain departments and conducting periodic surveys.
- Optimize Recruitment Channels by investing more in Indeed and LinkedIn while reassessing underperforming sources.
- Career Development Programs to reduce turnover caused by “career change” and “more money” motivations.
- Data Monitoring Automation — maintain real-time Power BI dashboards for HR to track workforce trends monthly.
